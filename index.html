Our national flag

red

the top band is of Saffron color, which indicates the strength and courage of the country. Our leaders must be unique and away from material gains and dedicate themselves to their work

white

The white band middle band indicates peace and truth with Dharma Chakra. The white color denotes, the path of truth to guide our conduct. The last band is green in color shows auspiciousness of the land.

green

The green color shows our relation to plant life, on which all other life depends. Ashok Chakra at the center of the white strip denotes the law of dharma.
